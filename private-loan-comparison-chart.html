Total Cost Accumulation: $10,000 Loan with Immediate Repayment

How your total payment grows over the life of each loan

This chart shows how the total cost of three different loans accumulates over time. Lender A reaches $14,245 total cost after 10 years. Lender B has two scenarios: at the minimum 5.5% rate, it reaches $15,000 after 15 years; at the maximum 12% rate, it reaches $22,032 after 15 years. Lender C reaches $15,596 after 10 years. The chart illustrates how Lender B's longer repayment term and variable rate create both extended payment obligations and significant cost uncertainty.
